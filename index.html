About Me

I am Sasuke Uchiha, one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha clan. My goal has always been to avenge my family and rebuild my clan. With the power of the Sharingan and my relentless pursuit of strength, I have walked a path of vengeance, rivalry, and redemption

Skills

  • Sharingan and Mangekyō Sharingan mastery
  • Chidori and lightning-based techniques
  • Mastery of the Susanoo
  • Expert in Kenjutsu (swordsmanship)
  • Strategic and analytical thinking in battle

Professional Experience

1. Obtaining the Mangekyo Sharingan

After confronting and defeating my older brother, Itachi Uchiha, I unlocked the Mangekyō Sharingan. This event was a turning point in my journey, revealing the truth about Itachi's sacrifices and deepening my resolve.

2. Defeating Danzo Shimura

In my quest for justice, I confronted Danzo Shimura, who was responsible for the massacre of the Uchiha clan. In a fierce battle, I defeated him and solidified my power, furthering my reputation as a formidable shinobi.
